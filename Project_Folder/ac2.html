Escape from Alcatraz 2.0

In the year 2045, Alcatraz Island had been repurposed into a high-tech prison for the world's most dangerous criminals. Jake "The Phantom" Reynolds, a master thief, found himself trapped in this fortress after a heist went wrong. Jake had a plan. He’d been gathering intel for months, bribing guards, and mapping out escape routes. The night finally came. Using a makeshift lockpick, he escaped his cell and navigated through the labyrinthine corridors. Security drones patrolled the halls. Jake used a hacked device to disrupt their signals, slipping past them. He reached the control room, where he uploaded a virus to disable the island’s security systems temporarily. Alarms blared as Jake made his way to the dock. Guards were on high alert. Using stealth and speed, he avoided confrontations, but time was running out. He reached the dock just as the security systems rebooted. A boat was waiting, driven by his partner in crime, Lisa. “Let’s go!” she shouted. They sped away, with bullets splashing in the water around them. Alcatraz’s defenses were formidable, but they managed to evade pursuit. As they reached the open sea, Jake breathed a sigh of relief. “We did it,” he said, looking back at the island. Lisa smiled, “But where to next?” Jake grinned, “Anywhere but here.”
